ACME Storm Cloud Catalogue



Rain Cloud

This is a general purpose rain cloud. Lots of water, but a very low electrical output makes this cloud safe for all ages. (1 Megawatt)

Storm Cloud

This is a high output storm cloud. Loud thunder, bright lightning and high electrical output. Not recommened for children. (100 Megawatts)

Funnel Cloud

More wind than rain. Little to no electrical output, but incredible cyclone action. Winds up to 300 kph (100 watts)

Dust Cloud

No water output, but kicks up a hefty dust and debrise wind storm. Dust particles have a high static charge. (100 milliwatts)
