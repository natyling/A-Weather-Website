Why Use ACME Storm Clouds?

Here are some of the more popular uses
for personal storm clouds ...

  • crop & lawn watering

  • shade on demand

  • extinguish fires

  • avoid outdoor events such as picnics, car washing, or mowing the lawn by creating a rain storm

  • portable electricity (requires optional Lightning-to-AC converter)

  • rinse car or house

  • thunder and lightning on demand - allows you to make a dramatic entrance

  • natural source of clean drinking water

  • entertainment - bumper clouds (requires optional action controlers)

  • snow in the winter, or use the optional freezer attachment for year-round snow

... and much more
